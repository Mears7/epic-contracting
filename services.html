Siding

Epic is one of the larger siding contractors in Saskatoon for a reason. Our employees are trained to install all of the popular exterior siding options.

‍James Hardie fibre cement siding

We specialize in using fibre cement for housing exteriors. It can be harder to handle and install but for exteriors of houses, it’s more durable, it’s fire-resistant, and it results in a more finished look.

Exterior insulation

Exterior insulation can help make older homes more energy-efficient and can be applied to almost any existing house. Exterior foam insulation is installed on the exterior side of the wall framing, and then the finish siding is added.

Vinyl siding

Vinyl is one of the most affordable and versatile options for siding. It comes in every colour of the rainbow, and in Saskatoon, vinyl siding stands up to the tough weather conditions we deal with here.

Metal siding

Virtually maintenance-free, metal can be installed on almost any type of building and provides a finish that lasts longer than most other kinds of siding — a great choice especially for commercial projects. 

ACM panels

Aluminum composite material panels are a wall cladding panel system with a modern look. This fade-resistant, weather-resistant material looks great on contemporary houses and on commercial buildings.

Steel siding

While steel siding may not be available in as many styles and colours as vinyl or aluminum, it is one of the most durable types of siding, lasting up to 30 years. 

Doors and windows

Epic started as a door and window company in Saskatoon in 2004 and we’re still doing window and door installation in Saskatoon today. We install all types of windows and doors, including exterior door installation for residential homes and commercial doors and windows.

Metal cladding for doors and windows

Wooden framed doors and windows will eventually dry out and rot. With metal cladding, custom-fabricated aluminum fits over existing wood frames, providing years of protection for a lower price than new doors and windows. 

Soffit, fascia and eavestroughs

Whether you need help with commercial eavestroughs or work on your home, Epic is the team you should work with for soffit fascia and eavestroughs in Saskatoon. 

While soffit, fascia and eavestroughs can be easy to overlook, they’re important to the structural integrity of homes. Eavestroughs reroute water and debris away from your house, soffit provides ventilation to reduce moisture and fascia caps it all off with a clean look.
