About us

Epic Contracting and Epic Exteriors are locally-owned, family-run businesses that have been operating in Saskatoon and the surrounding area since 2004. We started doing doors and windows in Saskatoon but soon expanded to become a general contracting company that provided services for housing exteriors, commercial siding, commercial eavestrough, and more — offering complete exterior work.

Amongst Saskatoon siding companies, Epic stands out for the quality of work we provide and the friendly team that serves you. When you choose us, you work directly with Epic owners Vern Epp and Brett Epp, and with our team of employees who we’ve trained to our high standards in contracting services. We take the time to listen, and we deal with our customers on a one-to-one basis. 

We pride ourselves on our high-quality workmanship and fair, competitive pricing. We work closely with our customers to ensure your vision for your home or commercial property is brought to completion.

Epic is fully licenced and insured, and a member of the Saskatoon & Region Home Builders’ Association. We have professional standards and installation whether we’re tackling home exterior renovations or commercial construction work, and we guarantee customer satisfaction with every job. 

We are a hard-working and dedicated general contractor in Saskatoon. Our satisfied customers are very important to us and have always been our best advertising.

Visit our showroom at 1901 1st Avenue North to see our products and discuss the contracting services you need.
